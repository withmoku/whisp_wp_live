== Description ==

WHISP sends leads’ purchasing details directly to your sales agent’s mobile phone allowing them to start the conversation with context. All enriched data is passed to your CRM where you can setup an automated sequence.

Lead Capture Solved

The current lead capture process is broken because it doesn’t
centralize around unique identifier.The CELL number.

Making the world a better place through elegant lead capture technology.
